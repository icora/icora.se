About

Icora is a venture company driven by entrepreneurs for entrepreneurs. We strive to make the greatness in your business even greater by implementing experience, funding and a global network.

We are always looking to find the next big thing. We are small but on top of our game with early stage investments up to 500 000 usd per engagement.

Through all the years we been around as entrepreneurs we have established an extraordinary network of competence to be able to really engage and put value into the companies we invest in.

In all the companies we get involved with we put in our hearts and souls to get the potential of the companies to burst into success. When we do something we do it all the way.
