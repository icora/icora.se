What we want

We want hungry, visionary entrepreneurs that have both a realistic expectation of the market they are in and also the ability to get things done.

The business model has to be scalable and appeal to a global market.

The team behind the business is a key component to get a successful business going so we find that extremely important to check of our list of qualifications required.

The business needs to have the factors in place to separate it self from the rest of the competitors, to clearly define it self as the alternative of choice.
